Swedish Meatballs

Description

Swedish Meatballs are super tasty, and pretty easy to make in a crockpot or related cooking manner. Great for parties!

Ingredients

  • Meatball
  • Salt
  • Sesame Oil
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat up crockpot.
  2. Season the balls.
  3. Put the balls in the crockpot.
  4. Put the lid on the crockpot.
  5. Wait for the balls to cook.
  6. Enjoy!
