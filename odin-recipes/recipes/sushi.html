Sushi

Description

I'm personally a sweet potato roll guy, alongside spicy salmon, yellowtail, or tuna rolls. Shrimp tempura is also a solid option!

Ingredients

  • Tobiko
  • Fish
  • Seaweed
  • Rice

Directions

  1. Cook your rice.
  2. Position rice on seaweed.
  3. Put raw cut fish in seaweed.
  4. Add tobiko to the outside of the rice, alongside unagi sauce.
  5. Platter and enjoy!
