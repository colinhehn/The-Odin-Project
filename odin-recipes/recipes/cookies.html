Cookies

Description

Cookies are the bomb, who doesn't like cookies????

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Chocolate

Directions

  1. Heat up oven.
  2. Mix the ingredients.
  3. Put the dough on a cookie sheet.
  4. Put the cookie sheet in the oven.
  5. Wait for the cookies to cook.
  6. Enjoy!
